Chili with Tofu

There are a lot of interesting legends and myths about the origin and before you know it, you have fallen into the chili-Rabbit hole. From Spanish nuns, to Canarian emigrants, prison, cowboy and soldier catering to precursors of today's food trucks, the so-called "Chili Queens". There is guaranteed to be an amusing story for everyone, you can read it - here


Ingredients

Preparation time 20
Working time 30 min

For the chili with with Tofu

  • 450 g Tofu Natural
  • 3 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 onion
  • 2 clover of garlic
  • 1 red chili
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp rapeseed oil
  • 250 g corn-cooked
  • 250 g kidney beans-cooked

For the sauce

  • 300 mlTomato pasta
  • 200 mlvegetable broth
  • 2 tbsptomato paste
  • 4 tspcumin
  • 2 tspagave syrup

In addition

  • 1 handful of coriander
  • 1 lime
  1. Finely "crumb" tofu in a bowl with your hand. Add garlic powder, salt and chili powder and mix well
  2. Finely chop the onion, garlic and chili, dice the peppers
  3. Put oil in a hot coated pan and fry onion, garlic, pepper and chili over medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring more often
  4. Add tofu and fry well for another 5 minutes over medium to high heat. Stir from time to time
  5. Put the ingredients for the sauce in a saucepan and bring to a boil briefly. Add all the ingredients from the pan and simmer for 20 minutes over medium heat with the lid closed
  6. Add corn and kidney beans and simmer for another 5 minutes
  7. Season with salt and pepper and serve tortilla chips or fresh bread, lime wedges coriander

Tips


The chili can be picked up very well and tastes even better.
